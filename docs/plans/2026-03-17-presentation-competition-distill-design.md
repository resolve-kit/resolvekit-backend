# Presentation Competition Distill Design

**Goal:** Reduce the presentation `Competition` chapter to its core essence while keeping visible price indication.

## Core Purpose

This section should answer one question quickly: why ResolveKit is different from incumbents.

## Distill Decision

- Keep the section heading on the left.
- Replace the current visible right side with one compact card only.
- The visible card contains:
  - one sentence: `Incumbents sell helpdesk automation. ResolveKit owns in-app resolution.`
  - one short pricing block:
    - `Public band: $0.80-$1.50+`
    - `ResolveKit target: $0.20`
- Move all explanatory comparison detail and vendor-by-vendor evidence behind the disclosure.

## Content Standard

- No repeated position statements.
- No visible comparison columns.
- No visible vendor explanation paragraph.
- Price should be visible at a glance.
- The visible section should read in under five seconds.
